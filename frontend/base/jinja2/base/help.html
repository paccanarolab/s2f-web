Submitting a new experiment

Prediction alias

A short name to recognize your experiment later.

Add an email if you want notifications when the job starts or finishes.

FASTA file

Upload your protein sequences (up to 5000). These sequences will be used to predict functions.

Functional annotations (optional)

TSV/TSV-like tab-separated file: first column protein identifier, second column GO term. Used as ground truth to enforce known annotations.

Understanding results

Finished jobs provide a downloadable TSV file. Each row contains:

  • Protein – the sequence identifier.
  • GO annotation – predicted Gene Ontology term.
  • Score – confidence of the prediction.

Open the TSV in a spreadsheet or script it to filter by score or specific GO terms.
