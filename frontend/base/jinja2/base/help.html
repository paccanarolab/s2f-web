Submitting a new experiment

Prediction alias

A short name for your experiment. Use it to recognize your submission later.

Email (optional)

Provide an email if you want notifications when the job starts or finishes.

FASTA file

Upload your protein sequences (up to 5000). Each sequence will be used for function prediction.

Functional annotations (optional)

Tab-separated file: first column protein identifier, second column GO term. Used as ground truth to enforce known annotations.

Checking job status

After submission, you receive a token. Go to Job Status, paste the token, and view progress. You can also follow the direct job link provided after submission.

Understanding results

When finished, you can download a TSV file. Each row contains:

  • Protein – the sequence identifier.
  • GO annotation – predicted Gene Ontology term.
  • Score – the prediction confidence.

You can open the TSV in spreadsheet software or process it programmatically to filter by score or terms.
